"Haya by Rabi" stands as a testament to empowerment and self-sufficiency. The mission is clear: to provide meaningful employment opportunities for disadvantaged women in Pakistan, particularly those affected by the pandemic. Focusing on Islamic clothing, Tasbeehs, and handmade products, the brand preserves cultural heritage and promotes the resurgence of "Made in Pakistan" goods globally.

I played a significant role in developing the "Haya by Rabi" application, demonstrating professionalism and expertise in Android development. Through seamless integration of functionality and aesthetics, I contributed to realizing the brand's vision. The CEO's review highlighted my dedication and proficiency, emphasizing the quality of my work. As reflected in the feedback, my contribution surpassed expectations, leaving a lasting impression of reliability and excellence.
