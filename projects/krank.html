As the Lead Android Developer at Krank for nearly four years, I played a pivotal role in the development of the revolutionary Krank Android App. My expertise in Java and Kotlin allowed me to meticulously craft code that seamlessly integrated various SDKs, including Agora and Zoom, enhancing the app's functionality. My commitment to excellence in app development significantly contributed to Krank's success in transforming equipment remarketing and elevating business dynamics.
